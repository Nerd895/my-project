Here's the last steps:

  • Cook the pasta: Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve ½ cup of pasta water, then drain.

  • Infuse the oil: In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook gently until garlic is golden—don’t let it burn!

  • Combine: Add the drained pasta to the skillet. Toss well to coat in the garlic oil. Add a splash of reserved pasta water to loosen if needed.

  • Finish: Stir in parsley and cheese (if using). Toss again and serve immediately.
