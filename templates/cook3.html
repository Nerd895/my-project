Here's the last steps:

  • Roast the eggplant: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss eggplant slices with olive oil and salt. Roast in a single layer for ~40 minutes until golden. Cool and reduce oven to 325°F (160°C).
  • Prepare the filling: Whisk together feta, cream cheese, eggs, cream, and pepper until smooth.
  • Assemble: Line a deep 8-inch pan with foil and drizzle with olive oil. Arrange eggplant slices upright, leaning against each other. Fill gaps with tomatoes and sprinkle half the oregano.
  • Bake: Pour the cheese mixture over the vegetables, leaving some exposed. Top with remaining oregano. Bake for 35–55 minutes until set and golden.
  • Finish: Drizzle with olive oil and za’atar. Serve warm or at room temperature.
